Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Kidney beans
  • Black beans
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Shredded cheese (for topping)
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, saut√©ing until translucent.
  2. Add ground beef to the pot, cooking until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper, cooking for another minute to release the spices' flavors.
  4. Add diced tomatoes (with their juice), kidney beans, and black beans to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  5. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce heat to low. Cover and let it cook for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Adjust seasoning as needed. If you prefer a thicker chili, let it simmer uncovered for an additional 10-15 minutes.
  7. Serve hot, topped with shredded cheese and fresh cilantro.
