Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, sautéing until translucent.
  3. Add ground beef to the skillet, cooking until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  6. Finish with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve warm.
